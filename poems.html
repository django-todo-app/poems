A poem about Indian Freedom Fighters

Oh heroes, heroes of India's past,
Yer courage and bravery shall forever last.
Yer names will never be forgotten, Yer fought fer our freedom, every drop o' sweat was worth it.

Oh heroes, heroes of India's past,
Yer names will be remembered and forever last.
Yer courage and bravery will never die,
Yer fought fer independence, no matter the odds and the lie.

Oh heroes, heroes of India's past,
Yer courage and strength will forever last.
Yer sacrifices and valor will always be remembered,
Fer us, ye broke the chains and our freedom was regained.

Oh heroes, heroes of India's past,
Yer names will live on and forever last.
Yer spirit of courage and bravery will never die,
India salutes ye and thanks ye fer lightin' the fire o' freedom in the sky!
